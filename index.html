MindMate RAG Project [Mental Health Domain]

Feb, 2025 | Generative AI

My current LLM post-training experience revolves around the MindMate project, which enhances chatbot responses in the mental health domain using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). This project focuses on developing and evaluating three open-source LLMs—Mistral-7B-v0.3, Meta-Llama/Llama-3.1-8B, and Tiiuae/Falcon3-7B-Instruct—to ensure chatbot interactions are contextually accurate, ethically responsible, and supportive. Implemented on Streamlit, the chatbot is trained using mental health publishments such as The Body Keeps the Score, Fundamentals of Psychology, and Trauma and Recovery, providing informed and reliable responses. The project aims to break the stigma around mental health by creating a private, judgment-free space, offering free, on-demand access, and assisting users in self-assessment to determine whether professional help is needed. Through this work, I am gaining hands-on experience in fine-tuning LLMs, optimizing chatbot interactions, and ensuring AI-driven responses align with ethical guidelines, integrating advanced NLP techniques with responsible AI to contribute to a more accessible and supportive digital mental health landscape.
